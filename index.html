A little About Me

My life is my playground

I graduated from MIT in 2011, where I studied Electrical Engineering and Computer Science with minors in Creative and Technical Writing. I worked at Microsoft for 5 years as a program manager on Windows & Windows Phone. I consider himself a geek/developer/student/entrepreneur. Given this is an ever-changing world, I must keep learning, embrace ambiguity, and find the positive outcome of chaos, in order to move forward into the future.


Helping everyone reach their full potential is what drives me in life. I want the world to be a place where all citizens are aspired and self-efficient, a place where the government focuses on the well-being of its citizens, a place that focuses and solves real world problems. This led me to my passion to pioneer the future. I believe the sky is NOT the limit. Our imaginations are... I plans to leverage this believe and passion into something that can help others grow.
